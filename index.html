About us

GKD Slice of Pizza

Welcome! We are passionate about creating delicious, authentic, and high-quality pizzas that satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more. Our mission is to provide our customers with an exceptional dining experience that is both affordable and enjoyable. Our pizza store uses only the freshest and finest ingredients, sourced locally and internationally, to create the perfect blend of flavors in each pizza. We believe in using only the best ingredients to ensure that every bite of our pizza is bursting with flavor. Our skilled pizza makers are trained in the art of pizza-making and use traditional techniques to ensure that our pizzas are cooked to perfection. Our dough is made fresh every day, and we use a wood-fired oven to cook our pizzas, which gives them that distinctive, crispy crust that we all love. We serve pizza to our beloved customers since 1995.
